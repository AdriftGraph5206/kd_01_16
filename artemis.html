Temple of Artemis at Ephesus

  • Location: Ephesus (present-day Turkey)
  • Built for: Artemis, the Greek goddess of the hunt
  • Construction period: Completed around 550 BC, rebuilt circa 350 BC after a fire
  • Height: Approximately 18 meters (59 feet)
  • Material: Marble
  • Notable features: A large temple dedicated to Artemis, renowned for its size and ornate sculptures.
