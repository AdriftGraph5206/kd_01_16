Hanging Gardens of Babylon

  • Location: Babylon, Iraq (exact location is disputed)
  • Built for: King Nebuchadnezzar II's wife, Amytis
  • Construction period: Around 600 BC
  • Height: Details unknown, often described as terraced gardens
  • Material: Unknown, possibly mud brick and terracotta
  • Notable features: A terraced garden with exotic plants and trees; existence and actual location debated among historians.
