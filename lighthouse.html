Lighthouse of Alexandria

  • Location: Alexandria, Egypt
  • Built for: To guide sailors into Alexandria's harbor
  • Construction period: Around 280 BC
  • Height: Approximately 100-130 meters (330-430 feet)
  • Material: Limestone and granite
  • Notable features: A towering lighthouse, one of the tallest man-made structures of its time, with a mirror that reflected sunlight during the day and a fire at night.
