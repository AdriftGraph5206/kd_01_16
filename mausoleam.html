Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

  • Location: Halicarnassus (present-day Bodrum, Turkey)
  • Built for: Mausolus, a Persian satrap, and his wife Artemisia
  • Construction period: Around 350 BC
  • Height: Approximately 45 meters (148 feet)
  • Material: White marble
  • Notable features: A monumental tomb with elaborate sculptures and friezes.
