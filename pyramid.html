Great Pyramid of Giza

  • Location: Giza, Egypt
  • Built for: Pharaoh Khufu (Cheops)
  • Construction period: Around 2600 BC
  • Original height: Approximately 146.6 meters (481 feet)
  • Current height: Approximately 138.5 meters (454 feet)
  • Base length: About 230.3 meters (755.6 feet)
  • Material: Limestone, mortar, granite
  • Notable features: The Great Pyramid contains three known chambers and remains the largest pyramid in Egypt.
